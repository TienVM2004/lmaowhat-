Ly do co cuoc thi Hackathon: Thuc hien muc tieu lan rong niem dam me lap trinh, tao dong luc cho sinh vien ap dung cong nghe vao thuc tien, vien tri tue nhan tao xay dung ke hoach trien khai cuoc thi Hackathon lap trinh tro choi ung dung cho giao duc "Game hoa giao duc" (gamify education) danh cho sinh vien cac truong Dai hoc, va hoc sinh cac truong THPT tren dia ban Thanh pho Ha Noi.Muc dich, y nghia cua cuoc thi Hackathon: Phat huy suc manh cua tuoi tre Vien Tri tue nhan tao - Dai hoc Cong nghe, Dai hoc Quoc gia Ha Noi, dac biet phat huy cac the manh ve hoat dong chuyen mon.
